Welcome to SignaScribe - A Real Time Sign Language Recognition Software

SignaScribe is an advanced web application designed to break communication barriers and foster inclusive interactions for individuals using sign language. Our software combines cutting-edge technologies, including convolutional neural networks (CNN) and OpenCV, to provide real-time recognition of sign language gestures.
Our Vision:
At SignaScribe, we envision a world where effective communication is accessible to everyone, regardless of their communication preferences or abilities. We are committed to harnessing the power of technology to bridge the gap between sign language users and the wider community.
Key Features:
  • Real-Time Recognition: Say goodbye to delays! With SignaScribe's real-time recognition capabilities, you can experience prompt and accurate interpretation of sign language gestures on the fly.

  • User-Friendly Interface: We understand the importance of simplicity. SignaScribe boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface that makes communication effortless for all users.

  • Seamless Integration: Whether you're using a desktop, tablet, or smartphone, SignaScribe's responsive design ensures a seamless experience across all devices.
How it Works:
Using SignaScribe is as easy as 1-2-3! Simply click the "Start Recognition" button, and our application will open a video feed to input your sign language gestures. Our powerful recognition engine will analyze the video frames in real-time, providing you with immediate and accurate results.


Get started with SignaScribe today and unlock the power of seamless sign language recognition!
